How to Play Simon Color Game

Overview

The Simon Color Game is a classic electronic game that was first introduced in 1978. It is a memory game that tests your ability to remember and repeat a sequence of colors and sounds.

Gameplay

The game consists of four colored buttons: red, blue, green, and yellow. The game starts with the computer flashing one of the buttons in a random sequence. Your goal is to repeat the sequence by pressing the buttons in the correct order.

  1. Press the start button to begin the game.
  2. Watch and listen carefully as the computer flashes the colors in a sequence.
  3. Repeat the sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order.
  4. If you get the sequence right, the computer will add another color to the sequence.
  5. If you get the sequence wrong, the game is over.
  6. Continue playing until you reach the maximum level or lose the game.

Scoring

The score is based on the level you reach. Each level adds one more color to the sequence, making it harder to remember. The game is over once you make a mistake.

Tips and Tricks

  • Pay attention to the sequence of colors and sounds.
  • Repeat the sequence out loud to help you remember it.
  • Start with a short sequence and gradually increase the length as you improve.
  • Take breaks to avoid fatigue and improve your concentration.
